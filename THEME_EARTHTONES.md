## ğŸ¯ Design Philosophy

The earth tone theme was chosen to:

1. **Reduce Eye Strain**: Warm, muted colors are easier on the eyes than bright blues
2. **Natural Feel**: Earth tones create a more organic, comfortable workspace
3. **Professional Look**: Brown and tan combinations convey stability and reliability
4. **Better Contrast**: Carefully selected shades ensure readability
5. **Cohesive Palette**: All colors work harmoniously together
